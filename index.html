Ear Check

Test your earphones & headphones – left, right & center channels

How to use:
  1. Put on your earphones or headphones.
  2. Click Left, Center or Right above to test that channel instantly.
  3. You should hear the sound only in the indicated ear (or both for Center).
  4. If you hear sound in the wrong ear, your left/right channels may be swapped.
  5. Scroll down to adjust the sound type and volume per channel.
